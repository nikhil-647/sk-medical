About Us

SK Medical Services is a reputed company in the Medical Diagnostic Equipment industry. We specialize in GE refurbished and pre-owned equipment like CT, MRI, PET CT, Ultrasound, X-Ray, and more. Established in 2017, we are committed to offering affordable, high-quality solutions to support healthcare in remote areas.
