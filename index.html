About SK Medical Services

A trusted name in medical diagnostic equipment since 2017

Our Story

SK Medical Services is a reputed company in the Medical Diagnostic Equipment industry. Promoted by highly skilled professionals, we deal in GE refurbished and pre-owned Diagnostic Imaging Equipment such as CT, PET CT, Nuclear Medicine, MRI, Cath Lab, AW Workstation, Mammography, C-Arm, Ultrasound and BMD.

We also offer turnkey after-sales service and quality spare parts for this equipment. Another key differentiator that sets us apart is our long-standing association and partnerships with other leading companies across the world to procure spare parts for GE Diagnostic Imaging equipment.

Our Vision

To help support vision of providing quality healthcare in remote areas of country by extending life span of old imaging equipment and thus offering lower costs for imaging equipment.

Company History & Milestones

Our journey of growth and partnerships in the medical equipment industry

2017


SK Medical Services established as sales and service provider of Radiology and Imaging equipment's of multiple modalities like CT Scan, Cathlab, MRI, C-arm, CR System and X-ray etc.

2018


Started working as Sales & Service partner for UNICARE MEDICAL SYSTEM for imported cardiac equipment's like heart Lung machine, Anesthesia, defibrillator with total Cardiac OT setup.

2019


Started working as sales & service partner for NEW GEN MEDICAL SYSTEM for PHILIPS MRI channel partnership in Mumbai.

2020


Started working as sales & service partner for Edge Medical Services for SIEMENS CT Scan & CATHLAB partnership in Maharashtra.

Started working as sales & service partner for SUNRAYS MASTERS MEDICAL SYSTEM for SIEMENS CATHLAB and Philips CT channel partnership in Maharashtra.

Bharat Electronics and AEONMED ventilator installation done at time of Covid-19 Lockdown Period in all over Maharashtra with 35,000 ventilator installations & still service support for ventilators on call facilities given to them.

Merged with Genesis Medical System, Aurangabad for Marathwada Region partner.
